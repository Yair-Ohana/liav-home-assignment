Discover TMDB

The Movie Database (TMDB) is a dynamic, community-driven platform dedicated to movies and TV shows. Since our inception in 2008, our extensive database has been enriched by contributions from an incredible community of users around the globe. Our commitment to internationalization and comprehensive data is unparalleled, making TMDB a unique hub for film enthusiasts and developers alike. At TMDB, community is at the heart of what we do, and it's what sets us apart.

Why Choose TMDB?

  • Community Contributions: Each year since 2008, we’ve seen a surge in contributions to our database. With over 1.5 million developers and organizations leveraging our platform, TMDB has established itself as a leading source for film and television metadata.
  • Vast Media Library: Beyond extensive information about movies, TV shows, and individuals, we pride ourselves on offering an impressive collection of high-resolution posters and backdrops. Our community adds more than 1,000 new images daily, ensuring our library remains fresh and exciting.
  • Global Reach: TMDB supports 39 languages and features extensive regional data, making it a truly global resource. Our platform is actively used in over 180 countries every day, bridging cultural gaps and uniting film lovers everywhere.
  • Unmatched Community Support: Our dedicated staff and community moderators are committed to providing exceptional support. We strive to ensure that your experience on TMDB is not just satisfactory but truly outstanding.
  • Reliable and Trusted: Millions of users depend on our platform daily, and we handle over 3 billion requests annually. With a proven track record, TMDB has established itself as a trustworthy resource for film and television information.
